The Climbing Club of Oulu and UIAA proudly present the super weekend of Ice Climbing: UIAA ICE CLIMBING World Youth Championships March 1–2 & European Cup Finals March 3 Also Finnish Championships take place.

The event venue is located roughly 10 km from the center of Oulu. The event is free of charge to spectators. The competition is broadcasted live and the highlights can be viewed afterwards from the compilation video.

COMPETITOR REGISTRATION: Ice Climbing World Youth Championships

NOTE: Athlete registration to UIAA should be done via national UIAA member federation. All climbers must also purchase a Competitor ticket via Eventbrite. Competitor ticket sale ends on Wednesday February 27 at 5pm (EET), and it is not possible to purchase the competition ticket on-site. Competitor ticket includes the competition fee, lunch and snacks at the competition site, side events and accommodation for 2 nights. NOTE: The accommodation cannot be guaranteed to those competitors who purchase their tickets after January 31! The competitors are also invited as spectators to the European Cup finals, including a lunch and closing party. If you are competing also in European Cup Finals, you need to buy a competitor ticket for that event, too.

COMPETITOR REGISTRATION: European Cup Finals

Athlete registration is done by purchasing competitor ticket. Competitor ticket sale ends on Saturday March 2 at 5pm (EET). It is not possible to buy the ticket on-site. Competitor ticket includes the competition participation fee, warm lunch on competition day, and access to official after party (sauna, food, live DJ, etc.). Competitors are also entitled to discounted accommodation (for more info, contact the organizers). CLIMBING COMMUNITY

FAQ - World Youth Championships


  • Athlete registration to UIAA should be done via national UIAA member federation. All climbers must also purchase a Competitor ticket.
  • Quota for each country is 6 girls and six boys
  • Age limit 16 -20 years
  • Competitor ticket sale ends on Wednesday 27th of February at 5pm (EET), and it is not possible to purchase the competition ticket on-site.
  • The accommodation cannot be quaranteed to those competitors who purchase their tickets after 31st of January
  • If you are competing in the European Cup Finals also, you will have to buy a competitor ticket for that event separately from www.finice2019.com.
  • Climbing community ticket is for coaches, family members, friends and climbing community to fully enjoy the Finice2019 and the side events, It covers both Word Youth Championships and European Cup Finals events as a spectator. With this ticket, you will get lunch at the competition site and entrance to the side events like closing parties of both competitions (including catering).
  • As a competitor you will get two nights free accommodation at Nallikari Holiday Village 28th of Febryary from 2nd of March). You or your team manager should contact the reception and organise how you you will stay. You can also book additional nights or add self-paying friends or family members to your group. www.nallikari.fi Tel. +358 44 703 1353 sales@nallikari.fi
  • VisitOulu’s website provides more information about Oulu and its services, and how to get here: http://www.visitoulu.fi/en

FAQ - European Cup


  • Registration: Buy ticket from Eventbrite
  • No country quota
  • Outside the continent – no points for European Cup

FAQ - Finnish Championships


  • Register by buying ticket from Eventbrite. If you are competing in World Youth Championships, remember to register also in Suomen Kiipeilyliitto.
  • No quota for Finnish climbers in the finals
  • Only lead for adults, also speed for youth.
